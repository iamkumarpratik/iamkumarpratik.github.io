Project information

  • Category: Web/Cloud Native Applications
  • Technologies: Python; JavaScript; AWS; PostgreSQL
  • Frameworks: Flask; FastAPI; VueJS.

Description

SenecaGlobal has a very quickly growing workforce, and they have a lot of custom rules and policies that are not very easy to find in the HRMS software available in the market. So they use their in-house applications to keep their data safe and have their own rules and policies being enforced in these applications.
